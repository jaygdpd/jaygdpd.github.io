 The process for designing this simple sticker actually turned out to be pretty complicated. First, I had to do brainstorm sketching, which evolved into intermediate and then final sketching. When I had a finished idea, I modeled it in Illustrator. The illustrator file was then exported into the Vinyl Master Cut Software, which cut the design into the vinyl material. Afterwards, I had to use an X-Acto blade, pick, and scissors to pick out the cut sticker from the rest of the vinyl. Then I transfered the sticker to transfer tape and stuck the vinyl on the tape with the help of a squeegee.
