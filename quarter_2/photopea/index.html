Photopea: Fun with Fonts

Photopea is one of those web-based Photoshop imitations that you don't typically have high expectations for, but I was impressed with how faithful it was to the Photoshop interface and workflow. The demo for Photopea involved using layer blending styles on texts, and impresively the various layer styles behaved exactly like their Photoshop counterparts. I wrote my name three times, and stylized it with shadows, glows, strokes, and color overlays. This was a really light assignment, and it was really just to make sure that we knew what Photopea was in the contingency case that we have to go online again for school.
