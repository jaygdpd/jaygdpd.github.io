Reflection

Overall, I have mixed feelings about how the cards came out. I loved my brainstorm ideas, but diverged significantly from them during my Illustrator process. Then, it took a long time to get them printed, and once they were printed I found the ink quality to be less than satisfactory. Nontheless, I proceeded with using the hydraulic cutter to cut the cards, and they came out okay. There were some cards that had extra bits hanging on them. But overall, the process wasn't too difficult, but I did have to make some pretty heavy revisions halfway through it.
