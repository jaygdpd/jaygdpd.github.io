Principles of Design Activity

Lines
This image shows a lit object against a dark backdrop. The strong lighting on the object emphasises it against the shadows outside the light.

Color
This image shows rhythm in the repetition of the rings. The rhythm shown here is gradated, as the rings get larger the further they are repeated from the center.

Space
This image contains asymmetrical balance. There is a white dot on one side of the line, yet the other side contains nothing but the blue backdrop. Thus, the image is weighted more towards the bottom left side of the image, making it asymmetrical.
