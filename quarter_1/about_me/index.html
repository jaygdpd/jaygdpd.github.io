About Me Poster

This About Me Poster is the first assignment in GDPD, and it's all about who we are. For my poster, I picked pictures that show my hobbies, activities I do, and things that I own. For example, my hobbies include playing Dungeons and Dragons (represented by the dice) and reading (represented by the Kindle). Things that I own in the poster include my mountain bike and my hand-built PC. I designed the layout of the poster to be like shattered glass in order to convey that these various images are all like shards of me, representative of me while only being a part of me.
