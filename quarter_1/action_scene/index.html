1. What selection tool did you use the most for this work? Why did you prefer to use it over the others? Explain.
I used quick select the most, since it was the most accurate and least time-consuming selection tool in my experience.
2. Explain what you photoshopped into each of your (3) backgrounds, and why you chose these subjects.
I photoshopped in giant animals, since I thought that that was the easiest way of introducing in a way of urgency and action into these otherwise peaceful and serene landscapes.
3. What part of this process did you find the most difficult?
Blending in these giant animals into the landscape without making it look too out of place was the most difficult part for me.
4. What is one thing that you think that you did a great job of in this work?
I did a great job at making the point clear in every picture. There's a giant monster, and its gonna eat you.
5. If you were asked to do this again, what would you do differently?
I would probably spend more time blending in these creatures into the landscape to make them look less out of place.
