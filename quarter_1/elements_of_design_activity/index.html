Elements of Design Activity

Lines
This image shows dark curved lines knotted together like a rope. The diagonal and curved lines here convey motion and action, creating a feeling of flow within the image.

Color
This image is composed primarily of light and warm colors. The use of these colors conveys friendliness, cheeriness, and a sense of warmth.

Space
This image is composed of two colors: black and white. Either color can be viewed as the primary color composing the shapes, with the other color producing negative spaces and shapes. For example, by viewing black as the foreground color, you can make out ovals composed of negative white space.
